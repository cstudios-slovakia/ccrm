This document outlines the core functions of the Laminam CRM, categorized for easy mapping to database schemas, UI components, or application logic.

1. Sales & CRM Core

Pipeline Management: Track, evaluate, and manage business opportunities and leads through the visual sales funnel.

Document Generator: Create, populate, and send template-based documents (e.g., proposals, quotes, contracts) automatically.

Online Booking: Automated appointment scheduling system with built-in two-way calendar synchronization.

2. Marketing & Lead Generation

Channel Analytics: Plan and track the ROI of different marketing channels to identify revenue-driving sources.

Dynamic Forms: Build and embed web forms to automatically collect leads and data directly into the CRM pipeline.

Newsletter Module: Send targeted email campaigns to clients and track open/click/conversion performance.

3. Project & Team Management

Task Management: Create tasks, assign owners, set deadlines, and track project progress using status boards.

Time Tracking: Log employee work hours and track time spent on specific projects, complete with reporting.

HR Management: Maintain employee records, track time-off/vacations, and handle performance evaluations.
